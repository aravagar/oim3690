Studioforma

The modular furniture manufacturing arm of my family's business group — premium wood-veneered doors, wall panels, and kitchen shutters, made in India.

Overview

Studioforma draws on more than 30 years of experience in the furniture industry to create chic, elegant designs. Headquartered in New Delhi, it serves clients throughout India and is built on the manufacturing backbone of Realply Industries.

The brand targets architects, interior designers, builders, and homeowners, offering five distinct series of doors alongside wall panels and kitchen shutters.

  • Doors Project, UV, Exclusive Design, PU Paint & Laminate series
  • Wall Panels Texture and character for modern interiors
  • Kitchen Shutters A wide range of designs and finishes
  • Made in India In-house manufacturing, E1-grade construction
